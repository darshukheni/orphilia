Microsoft Dynamic Services

Microsoft Dynamics NAV

Microsoft Dynamics NAV is an innovative Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system that automates and streamlines business processes. The comprehensive business solution provides small and midsize businesses control over financials, supply chain, production, project and other operations. It is a scalable, easy-to-configure, and simple-to-use business solution available on cloud and on premise.

Role Centre in Microsoft Dynamics NAV drives user productivity and enhances team collaboration in a visual and user-familiar environment. Web Client and Mobile Client further extend accessibility to business information without being constrained by location and device used.

Capability of Microsoft Dynamics NAV

Microsoft Dynamics NAV has the following basic and advanced modules.

  • Finance Management
  • Sales and Marketing Management
  • Purchasing Management
  • Inventory Management
  • Retail Management
  • Production Management
  • Quality Management
  • Maintenance Management
  • Service Management
  • Project Management
  • Rental Management
  • Warehouse Management
